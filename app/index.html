Expand Your Reach

You desire to see lives changed. As believers, we all do. But what's the most effective way to impact the lives in your city? What if there was a way to help more than just one or two ministries in your area, but a way to impact them all.

Through Mission Increase Foundation you can empower the ministries in your community to achieve a new level of excellence and sustainable growth. With MIF you leverage your giving to provide coaching, consulting, and matched grants to the ministries in your community - all for Kingdom impact. You do more than just give to one ministry, you expand your reach to make a radical change in the city you love.

Transform Your City

Think of the number of ministries in your city. Imagine how many lives they touch collectively. The families they change. The hope they bring. By partnering with MIF in your city you give each ministry an opportunity to grow.

Partner With MIF

Our desire is to find men and women who understand the power of leveraging a team to train multiple ministries for maximum results. When Mission Increase Foundation works in a city it advances numerous ministries, changing a multitude of lives.
